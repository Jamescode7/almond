JamesViewer bundles the following third-party assets for offline markdown rendering:

1. github-markdown-css v5.5.1
   License: MIT
   Source: https://github.com/sindresorhus/github-markdown-css
   Files:
     - Resources/css/github-markdown-light.css
     - Resources/css/github-markdown-dark.css

2. highlight.js v11.9.0
   License: BSD-3-Clause
   Source: https://github.com/highlightjs/highlight.js
   Files:
     - Resources/js/highlight.min.js
     - Resources/css/highlight-github.css (GitHub theme)
     - Resources/css/highlight-atom-one-dark.css (Atom One Dark theme)

Full license texts are available in the respective upstream repositories.
These assets are bundled to keep JamesViewer fully offline — no runtime CDN calls.
